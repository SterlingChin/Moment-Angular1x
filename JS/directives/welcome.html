Welcome to Moment!


Let's get started customizing Moment!

Mouseover the settings cog in the upper left corner. Type in your name and zipcode and click save. This will now change the greeting and update the weather.

In the settings menu you can toggle any one of the elements on or off. By default the 7 day forecast is toggled off. This is because you can reach the 7 day forecast by hovering over the current weather in the upper right hand corner and then clicking on the "show 7 day forecast" option.

Access the tutorial again by clicking the "?" in the bottom right.
