Welcome to Moment!


Thank you for adding Moment to your Chrome Extensions. What Moment gives you is complete control over your new tab environment. In the upper left hand corner you'll see the settings cog. In the settings menu you can toggle any one of the elements on or off. By default the 7 day forecast is toggled off. This is because you can reach the 7 day forecast by hovering over the current weather in the upper right hand corner and then clicking on the "show 7 day forecast" option.
